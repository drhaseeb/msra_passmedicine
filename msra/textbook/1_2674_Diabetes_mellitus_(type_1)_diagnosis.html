      Diagnostic criteria for type 1 diabetes mellitus<\/h5>
      If the patient is symptomatic:
      • fasting glucose greater than or equal to 7.0 mmol\/l<\/li>
      • random glucose greater than or equal to 11.1 mmol\/l (or after 75g oral glucose tolerance test)<\/li><\/ul>
        If the patient is asymptomatic the above criteria apply but must be demonstrated on two separate occasions.
