It is important to recognise as it may lead to ventricular tachycardia\/torsade de pointes<\/span> and can therefore cause collapse\/sudden death. The most common variants of LQTS (LQT1 & LQT2) are caused by defects in the alpha subunit of the slow delayed rectifier potassium channel<\/span>.
