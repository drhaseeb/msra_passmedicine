The term includes patients who have been labelled as having either impaired fasting glucose (IFG) or impaired glucose tolerance (IGT).         • impaired fasting glucose (IFG) - due to hepatic insulin resistance<\/li>
        • impaired glucose tolerance (IGT) - due to muscle insulin resistance<\/li>
        • patients with IGT are more likely to develop T2DM and cardiovascular disease than patients with IFG<\/li><\/ul>
