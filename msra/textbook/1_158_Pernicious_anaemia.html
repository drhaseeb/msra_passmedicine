Whilst pernicious anaemia is the most common cause of vitamin B12 deficiency<\/span>, it's not the only cause. Other causes include atrophic gastritis (e.g. secondary to H. pylori<\/i> infection), gastrectomy, malnutrition (e.g. alcoholism).
