Syntocinon<\/h5>
Syntocinon is a synthetic version of oxytocin that is used in the active management of third stage of labour<\/span>. It stimulates the contraction of the uterus reducing the risk of postpartum haemorrhage. It is also used to induce labour<\/span>.

Ergometrine<\/h5>
Ergometrine is an ergot alkaloid which is used as an alternative to oxytocin in the active management of third stage of labour<\/span>. By constricting vascular smooth muscle of the uterus it can decrease blood loss.
