            Chronic hepatitis C may be defined as the persistence of HCV RNA in the blood for 6 months.

            • rheumatological problems: arthralgia, arthritis<\/li>

            • cirrhosis<\/span> (5-20% of those with chronic disease)<\/li>
            • hepatocellular cancer<\/span><\/li>

              Management of chronic infection
              • treatment depends on the viral genotype - this should be tested prior to treatment<\/li>
              • the management of hepatitis C has advanced rapidly in recent years resulting in clearance rates of around 95%. Interferon based treatments are no longer recommended<\/li>
