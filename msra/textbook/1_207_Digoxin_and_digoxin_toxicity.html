      Plasma concentration alone does not determine whether a patient has developed digoxin toxicity. Toxicity may occur even when the concentration is within the therapeutic range<\/span>. The BNF advises that the likelihood of toxicity increases progressively from 1.5 to 3 mcg\/l.
