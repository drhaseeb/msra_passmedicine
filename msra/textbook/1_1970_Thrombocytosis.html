    Essential thrombocytosis<\/h5>
    Essential thrombocytosis is one of the myeloproliferative disorders which overlaps with chronic myeloid leukaemia, polycythaemia rubra vera and myelofibrosis. Megakaryocyte proliferation results in an overproduction of platelets.

    Features
    • platelet count > 600 * 109<\/sup>\/l<\/li>
    • both thrombosis (venous or arterial) and haemorrhage can be seen<\/li>
    • a characteristic symptom is a burning sensation in the hands<\/li>
    • a JAK2 mutation is found in around 50% of patients<\/li><\/ul>
