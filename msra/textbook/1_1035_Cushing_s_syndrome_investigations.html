          General findings consistent with Cushing's syndrome<\/h5>
          A hypokalaemic metabolic alkalosis<\/span> may be seen, along with impaired glucose tolerance.

          Ectopic ACTH secretion (e.g. secondary to small cell lung cancer) is characteristically associated with very low potassium levels.

          Tests to confirm Cushing's syndrome<\/h5>
          The three most commonly used tests are:
          • overnight (low-dose) dexamethasone suppression test<\/span>

            • patients with Cushing's syndrome do not have their morning cortisol spike suppressed<\/li><\/ul><\/li>
            • 24 hr urinary free cortisol
              • two measurements are required<\/li><\/ul><\/li>
              • bedtime salivary cortisol
                • two measurements are required<\/li><\/ul><\/li><\/ul>
