Selective oEstrogen Receptor Modulators (SERM)<\/h5>
Tamoxifen is a SERM which acts as an oestrogen receptor antagonist and partial agonist. It is used in the management of oestrogen receptor-positive breast cancer<\/span>.

Adverse effects
  • menstrual disturbance: vaginal bleeding, amenorrhoea<\/li>
  • hot flushes - 3% of patients stop taking tamoxifen due to climacteric side-effects<\/li>
  • venous thromboembolism<\/li>
  • endometrial cancer<\/li><\/ul>
